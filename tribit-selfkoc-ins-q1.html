Strategic Recommendations

  • Comment Crisis Response: Implement mandatory question-based captions. Every post must ask viewers a specific question. Target: 5+ comments per post within 30 days.
  • First Hour Strategy: All creators must engage with each other's posts within first 60 minutes. Like, comment, share, and save. This signals Instagram algorithm for broader distribution.
  • Reels-First Approach: 80% of content should be Reels format. Static posts average 127 views vs Reels at 892 views. Immediate format shift required.
  • March Success Replication: Analyze March content themes and replicate. 35% higher performance indicates seasonal opportunity or content type success.
  • Micro-Influencer Collaboration: Partner with 10-50K follower accounts in audio/tech space. Current reach suggests zero algorithmic favor.
  • Hashtag Overhaul: Current strategy failing. Research and implement 5 high-volume (1M+), 10 medium (100K-1M), and 15 niche (<100K) hashtags per post.
  • Story-to-Reel Funnel: Use Stories with polls/questions to drive Reel engagement. Story engagement doesn't show in metrics but drives algorithm signals.
  • Content Pillars Reset: Establish 3 core pillars: Product demos (40%), User testimonials (30%), Behind-the-scenes (30%). Current mix lacks focus.
  • Engagement Pods: Create internal engagement pod with all creators + 10 brand advocates. First 30 minutes engagement is crucial for reach.
  • Analytics Deep Dive: Weekly review of Instagram Insights. Identify exact posting times when audience is most active. Current random posting killing reach.
